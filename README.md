# 313D Refrigeration Training Data

Complete 313D Refrigeration Technician curriculum covering all aspects of commercial and industrial refrigeration systems.

## Overview

This repository contains the complete 313D Refrigeration Training Data used by the LARK Labs dev-tools MCP server for generating compliant refrigeration technician training content.

## Program Information

- **Program Code**: 313D
- **Program Name**: Refrigeration Technician
- **Version**: 1.0.0
- **Total Units**: 15 (14 Core + 1 Elective)
- **Total Hours**: 1,060 hours
- **Total Topics**: 192+

## Curriculum Structure

### Core Units (14 units, 1,010 hours)

1. **Unit 1**: Safety and Professional Standards (40 hours)
2. **Unit 2**: Tools and Equipment (60 hours)
3. **Unit 3**: Refrigeration Theory (75 hours)
4. **Unit 4**: Compressors (90 hours)
5. **Unit 5**: Condensers and Evaporators (70 hours)
6. **Unit 6**: Controls and Automation (80 hours)
7. **Unit 7**: Refrigerants and Oils (60 hours)
8. **Unit 8**: System Installation (100 hours)
9. **Unit 9**: Maintenance and Troubleshooting (90 hours)
10. **Unit 10**: Commercial Systems (80 hours)
11. **Unit 11**: Industrial Systems (70 hours)
12. **Unit 12**: Heat Pump Systems (65 hours)
13. **Unit 13**: Environmental Compliance (55 hours)
14. **Unit 14**: Advanced Topics (75 hours)

### Elective Units (1 unit, 50 hours)

- **Unit 15**: Specialty Refrigeration (50 hours)

## Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

- Understand refrigeration fundamentals and thermodynamics
- Install and maintain commercial and industrial refrigeration systems
- Diagnose and repair refrigeration equipment
- Apply comprehensive safety procedures
- Comply with environmental and regulatory requirements
- Perform advanced troubleshooting and system analysis
- Design and size refrigeration systems
- Manage and handle refrigerants safely

## Assessment Methods

- Knowledge tests and examinations
- Practical skills demonstrations
- Safety certifications
- System design projects
- Troubleshooting scenarios
- Competency evaluations

## Compliance Frameworks

All training content aligns with:

- **CSA Standards**: CSA B149 series, CSA C22 electrical standards
- **Environmental**: Environmental Protection Act (EPA), Ozone Protection regulations
- **Health & Safety**: Workplace Health and Safety Act (WHMIS), Safe handling procedures
- **Regulatory**: Provincial/federal licensing requirements, industry certifications
- **Professional**: HVACR technician licensing and competency standards
